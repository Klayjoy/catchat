About Cat Chat Tarot!

Welcome to Cat Chat Tarot; a vehicle for my tarot readings. I have been doing tarot readings as a hobby for more than twenty years and have decided to take my readings public. I exclusively use a tarot deck called: Mystical Cats Tarot, which incorporates the traditional tarot deck with a cat twist. I was endeared to this deck, because the cats featured in the deck are based on actual cats.

I really enjoy using this deck. If you are curious about Mystical Cats Tarot, you can check out their website here. It's the only deck I use for divination these days. I have used the deck enough to incorporate my own card meanings.

Although I do not publish my tarot readings online, I thought it would be fun to have a game where one can ask themselves a question, click on a button, and get an action card. You can find this game, on the Let's Play page.

If you would like to purchase a reading, I offer a variety of tarot readings styles, which can be purchased here. Thank you!
