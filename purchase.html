FAQ

  1. What type of payments do you take?

    At this point I only accept PayPal, but I am working on accepting other payments down the road.

  2. What if I have a 2-part question. Is that extra money?

    Depending on the reading that you purchase, it is one question per reading, but if the second question clarifies the main question I include it, as it gives the reading a more cohesive feel.

  3. Do I receive my reading via email or can I have it mailed to me?

    Either are fine. Just let me know.

  4. How long does it take to receive my reading?

    I generally work on requests within two days of receiving them via email, and then I corespond with querent so as to clarify their question. It is very important to ask a simple and properly stated question. I also refuse to do anything that has a fortune telling or predictive element to it.

  5. Do I get a discount if I purchase more than one reading?

    I offer a 10% discount for readings, with a price-point of $60 or more.
